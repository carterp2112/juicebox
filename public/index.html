Authentification

Tokens are required to authenticate.
Tokens are returned after a successful post to /api/users/login with an existing account, or a successful registration at /api/users/register.
Tokens are valid for a week.

Endpoints

/users

Allows you to view the users json.



/users/login

Allows you to login with a POST request.



/users/register

Allows you to register with a POST request.



/posts

Allows you to view posts json.
